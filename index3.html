To order any of our products...
  1. Click on the SHOP NOW button to view our catalogue
  2. Click on the add to cart button to add the item
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the screen where you'll find your cart
  4. Add as many items as you want by using the arrows beside your selected item in the cart.
  5. Add money to your wallet at the top right corner of the screen
  6. Then make your purchase and view your receipt at the bottom
